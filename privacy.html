Privacy Policy for YouTube Seek Buttons

Last updated: March 11, 2026

YouTube Seek Buttons is a Chrome extension that adds rewind and fast forward buttons to the YouTube video player.

Data Collection

YouTube Seek Buttons does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension stores only your preferred seek duration (in seconds) locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This value never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties.
